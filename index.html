About XI-D

A collection of allegedly intelligent humans slowly descending into academic madness.

Our "Prestigious" Journey

XI-D: Where they promised us brilliance, but forgot to mention we’d be held together by caffeine, sarcasm, and a GC that could end our futures if leaked. Between Shlok’s suspicious moans mid-conversation, Everyone's ongoing vendetta against Zainul, and Agam’s Olympic-level yapping, we’ve already got enough material for a Netflix dark comedy.

The PCM Experience

Classes are less about learning and more about chaos management. Sourik somehow remains calm while the rest of us spiral, Dheirya keeps leaning into his “acting gay” bit like it’s a full-time role, and Hammad… well, the school still hasn’t confirmed if he’s tall enough to legally be in Class XI. Somewhere in between, Physics, Chemistry, Math, and CS occasionally make cameo appearances.

Survival Strategy

We’ve mastered the art of pretending to know what’s going on—whether it’s a complex CS code or why Shlok just made that noise again. Coping methods include existential memes, roasting sessions, and the shared delusion that “we’ll figure it out.” Spoiler: we won’t. But we’ll have Agam narrating our downfall in real time.

The Reality Check

Beneath the madness, we’re weirdly efficient. We can debug code, fudge our way through lab work, and still have energy left to troll each other into insanity. We thrive on sarcasm, caffeine dependency, and a collective level of chaos that no curriculum could survive—kind of like how Hammad survives in a world built for people over four feet tall.
